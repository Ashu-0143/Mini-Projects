KURAMA

 
   

The Nine-Tailed Fox, known as Kurama, is one of the nine tailed beasts in the Naruto universe. It was first introduced in chapter 1 of the original Naruto manga, representing a powerful and ancient tailed beast creature. Kurama harbored great vengeance against the leaf village of Konoha and laid waste to it, eventually being sealed inside the baby son of Minato Namikaze and Kushina Uzumaki, who would grow up to be Naruto Uzumakii
